Our Academic Partners

Axial3D is committed to delivering excellence in medical 3D printing. We believe that Research and Development is one of the key factors in the successful delivery of this ambitious mission. Our collaborations with academic and research institutions, alongside the medical industry, assist in cohesive knowledge sharing, project development and enhanced understanding of new technologies, including AI and Machine Learning. We promote an open, collaborative and ideas-based atmosphere within our workplace and continually seek new and exciting opportunities for research and academic partnerships.
